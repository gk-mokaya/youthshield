Our Journey

Key milestones in Youth Shield Foundation growth and impact

October 2024

Early Days

Youth Shield was first started as program at the university of Eldoret with a mission to shield the youths especially students from drug abuse and exhibited during public lecture week held at the University of Eldoret from 16 - 18 October 2024.

January 2025

Foundation Launch

Early in January 2025 the youth shield program evolved into a foundation, expanding its cause and mission to the occupant of Eldoret town.

March 2025

Online Platform Launch

Launched our digital awareness and educational platform online, expanding access to worldwide and we able to sensitize 1,000 youths online within the first two months.

June 2025

Industry Partnerships

Established partnerships with NACADA and for the same cause of drug abuse awareness and digital education.

July 2025

Office Establishment

Established our first office at Eldoret town, Oloo street 64 Arcade, and continued to impact youths and women from all corners of the country.

January 2026

Registration in the Non Governmental Organization

Youth Shield Foundation was officially legally registered in the Non Governmental Organization.
